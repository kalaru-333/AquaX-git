Aqua X brings your aquarium to life with smart monitoring and AI insights. Stay informed, stay connected, and keep your fish healthy—anytime, anywhere.

Why AquaX matters?

Managing a fish tank is hard without constant monitoring.

Water changes, feeding schedules, and sudden parameter shifts can harm your fish—often before you even notice. We take the guesswork out of aquatic care.

How It Works?

Attach Aqua X to your tank, connect to the app, and enjoy smart, effortless aquarium care in three simple steps.

What AquaX Can Do For You?

💧

Water Quality Monitoring

Continuously measuring the temperature, pH levels, Temperature and Turbidity in real-time.

📷

Real Time Camera Footage

Smart camera technology streams a real time footage of the tank via the Aqua X App.

🚨

AI Health & Death Detection

Immediate alerts if a fish becomes completely unresponsive or passes away, protecting the tank ecosystem.
